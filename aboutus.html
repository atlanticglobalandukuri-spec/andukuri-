About Us

Welcome to Andukuri!

Andukuri is an online quiz-based platform where users can play quizzes and earn points by answering questions correctly. These questions based on Mathematics, Indian States & Capitals, and Alphabet Series. Each correct answer gives 1 point, while each wrong answer deducts 5 points, encouraging fair play and learning.

Our goal is to make learning fun and engaging through quizzes while rewarding users based on their performance. Andukuri is designed for users who enjoy testing their knowledge and improving their skills in a simple and user-friendly environment.

We believe in transparency, fairness, and providing a safe platform for everyone.
